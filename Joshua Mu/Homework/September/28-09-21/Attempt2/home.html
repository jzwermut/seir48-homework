Alan Turing's Hometown

Biography Trivia

Hometown

Alan Turing was born in Maida Vale, London, United Kingdom.

Interesting Things

  • The name derived from a pub called "The Maida"
  • The area was originally owned by the church.
  • Was home to a significant Sepharic Jewish district during the late 19th and 20th centuries. The point particularly emphasised by the way an 1878 magazine report referred to it as "New Jerusalem".
